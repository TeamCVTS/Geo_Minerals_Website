About Geo Minerals

Geo Minerals is a South African minerals processing and supply company specialising in foundry sand and chrome concentrate for chrome flour applications. The business is built on deep industry knowledge and practical expertise gained through continuous production activities for over 10 years.

The company focuses on delivering consistent, high-quality chrome sand that meets the demanding requirements of foundries and industrial users.

Vision

To be a trusted and respected supplier of high-quality foundry and chrome-based mineral products, recognised for technical excellence, reliability and long-term partnerships within the chrome industry.

Mission

Geo Minerals is committed to producing and supplying high-quality foundry sand and chrome concentrate that meet industry specifications and customer requirements.

  • Leveraging proven industry knowledge and production experience
  • Maintaining consistent quality control
  • Building long-term customer relationships
  • Operating responsibly within the minerals value chain

Industry Experience

The leadership and operational team behind Geo Minerals bring extensive practical experience in mineral processing and foundry material production. Active involvement in the industry since 2015 has enabled the company to develop a strong understanding of material performance, customer expectations and production quality requirements.
